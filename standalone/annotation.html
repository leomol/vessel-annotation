Outlining instructions

  • Outline the edge of the big blob at the center of each image. The first image may be already outlined (annotate this one too).
  • If half of the edge is blurry, assume a circular or an elliptical shape.
  • Don't include bright extrusions or bright dots right next to the main blob.
  • After the system accepts your submission, approval of your work will be decided later (< 2 weeks) by an expert and could potentially be rejected and re-posted. If your submission is rejected several times, you will be excluded from the platform.
  • Note for reviewers: This is a standalone version of the software for demonstration purposes only which prompts to load images and downloads the results after completing the task. The crowd-source version presents those images to the users and stores data automatically on the server.

Controls

  • This task requires using a desktop mouse with a scroll button.
  • Click on Browse to select a folder of images.
  • To change to another image, use the left/right arrow keys (faster) or click on Previous/Next.
  • To push markers outwards from their circular arrangement, click and hold inside the circle and move the mouse outwards.
  • To move the position of all the circularly arranged markers (circle tool), shift+click and drag the mouse.
  • To move individual markers, click and drag each marker.
  • To change the size of the circle tool, scroll down/up.
  • To reset the circle tool, make a right-click on the image.
  • The video tutorial below walks you through these steps.
